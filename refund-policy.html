Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: 14 February, 2026


Nature of Goods


Agricultural produce is perishable and sourced from independent Farmer-Vendors.


Cancellations


Orders may be cancelled before dispatch confirmation. 

Once dispatched, cancellation may not be possible.


Refunds


Refunds may be initiated if:


  • Order is not delivered

  • Produce is materially damaged upon delivery


Refunds will be processed within 7–10 working days to the original payment method. Refund eligibility may vary based on the nature of the issue and verification by the Farmer-Vendor and logistics partner.


Platform fees, if any, are non-refundable once service is rendered.
