Delete Your Prakrushi Account

If you wish to permanently delete your Prakrushi account, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email from your registered email address to:

contact@prakrushi.in

Subject line: Account Deletion Request

What Happens After Request

  • Your account will be deactivated within 7 working days.
  • Personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • Transaction records required for legal compliance may be retained as per applicable laws.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Profile information
  • Order history
  • Farmer participation data
  • App usage records linked to your account

If you face any issues, contact us at contact@prakrushi.in
